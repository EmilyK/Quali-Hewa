What is QualiHewa all about?

QualiHewa is a system that enables the monitoring of levels of air pollution in communities. Quali is derived from Quality. Hewa is a Swahili word for Air. Air Quality is what we aim to determine. A custom built air quality detector called The QualiHewa Analyser takes readings of different pollutants from the air and sends them to this platform. The data here is aggregated from different QualiHewa Analysers and presented in a simple and meaningful way. That is; inform of tables and charts. What you choose to do with this data is all up to you. We have provided a way for you to export this data and use it for different purposes. You can choose to view data for all stations or specific stations. A station is a place with a QualiHewa analyser.

Brief Background

One of the major public health and environmental concerns today is pollution. Air pollution is the introduction into the atmosphere of chemicals or biological materials that cause discomfort, disease, or death to humans, damage other living organisms such as food crops, or damage the natural environment or built environment. Air pollution has very adverse effects. According to the World Health Orgganisation, it is estimated that 3.5 million premature deaths occur every year due to household air pollution, and 3.3 million deaths due to outdoor air pollution. Pollution is rampant especially in urban areas of Uganda with cars and factories contributing to the high levels of pollution. As a result, it becomes essential to monitor the air for exceeding levels of pollutants such as Carbon Monoxide (CO), Sulphur Dioxide (SO2), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) so that appropriate actions can be taken. With this endeavor, we proposed the QualiHewa air pollution monitoring system that helps the society to monitor the levels of air pollution in the country. Personal air pollution monitoring stations owned by citizens across a location could create awareness about the composition of the air around them.
